USER GUIDE : 

To operate this inventory system 1st login to the data base & then operate software.

	> Mark Check to select the particular item the customer want.
	
	> Next is to enter the Quantity of that item which is opposite to it.

	> Next is to ENter the name of the Customer.

	> Next Step is to Enter the table no of the Customer.

	> Next Step is to assign the Order ID to the Customer

	> Now you can click on "make order" to make an order.

	> Next step is to show Receipt by clicking on it.

	> Now you can generate the bill by Printing it.
